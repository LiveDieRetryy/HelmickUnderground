GOOGLE ANALYTICS SETUP GUIDE
============================

Google Analytics will help you track:
- How many people visit your website
- Which pages they view
- Where they're located
- How they found you (Google search, direct, etc.)
- Contact form submissions

SETUP STEPS:
------------

1. CREATE GOOGLE ANALYTICS ACCOUNT:
   - Go to: https://analytics.google.com/
   - Sign in with your Gmail account (HelmickUnderground@gmail.com recommended)
   - Click "Start measuring"
   - Account name: "Helmick Underground"
   - Click "Next"

2. CREATE PROPERTY:
   - Property name: "Helmick Underground Website"
   - Time zone: (UTC-06:00) Central Time
   - Currency: US Dollar
   - Click "Next"

3. BUSINESS INFORMATION:
   - Industry: Construction/Contractors
   - Business size: Small (1-10 employees)
   - How you intend to use: "Examine user behavior"
   - Click "Create"
   - Accept Terms of Service

4. GET TRACKING CODE:
   - Select "Web" platform
   - Website URL: https://helmickunderground.com
   - Stream name: "Main Website"
   - Click "Create stream"
   
5. COPY YOUR MEASUREMENT ID:
   - You'll see a Measurement ID like: G-XXXXXXXXXX
   - Copy this ID

6. ADD TO YOUR WEBSITE:
   - Open index.html, about.html, services.html, contact.html
   - Add this code in the <head> section (after the <meta> tags):

   <!-- Google Analytics -->
   <script async src="https://www.googletagmanager.com/gtag/js?id=G-XXXXXXXXXX"></script>
   <script>
     window.dataLayer = window.dataLayer || [];
     function gtag(){dataLayer.push(arguments);}
     gtag('js', new Date());
     gtag('config', 'G-XXXXXXXXXX');
   </script>

   Replace G-XXXXXXXXXX with your actual Measurement ID

7. TRACK FORM SUBMISSIONS:
   - In script.js, add this inside the form success handler:
   
   gtag('event', 'form_submission', {
     'event_category': 'Contact',
     'event_label': 'Quote Request'
   });

WHAT YOU'LL SEE IN ANALYTICS:
------------------------------
- Real-time visitors on your site right now
- Daily/weekly/monthly visitor counts
- Most popular pages
- Geographic location of visitors
- Device types (mobile vs desktop)
- How long people stay on your site
- Bounce rate (people who leave immediately)

IMPORTANT METRICS FOR YOUR BUSINESS:
-------------------------------------
1. **Sessions** - Total visits to your site
2. **Users** - Unique visitors
3. **Conversion Rate** - % who submit contact form
4. **Top Pages** - Which services get most views
5. **Traffic Sources** - Google search, social media, direct

PRIVACY COMPLIANCE:
-------------------
You already have a cookie notice on your site ✓
Google Analytics is GDPR compliant when configured properly
Data is anonymized and doesn't collect personally identifiable info

VIEWING YOUR REPORTS:
---------------------
After setup, go to: https://analytics.google.com/
- Click "Helmick Underground Website"
- View "Reports" → "Realtime" to see live visitors
- View "Reports" → "Acquisition" to see where traffic comes from
- View "Reports" → "Engagement" to see popular pages

Check analytics weekly to see:
- Is traffic increasing?
- Which services are people interested in?
- Are people finding you through Google search?
- Where are most customers located?

Let me know your Measurement ID and I'll add the tracking code to all your pages!
