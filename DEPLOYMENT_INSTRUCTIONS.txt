STEP 2: ACCESS YOUR HOSTING
----------------------------
Since you own HelmickUnderground.com, you likely have hosting through:
- GoDaddy (most common)
- Hostinger
- Bluehost
- SiteGround
- Or another provider

To access your hosting:

FOR GODADDY:
1. Go to: https://www.godaddy.com/
2. Click "Sign In" (top right)
3. Enter your GoDaddy account credentials
4. Click "My Products"
5. Find "Web Hosting" and click "Manage"
6. Click "cPanel" or "File Manager"

FOR OTHER HOSTS:
1. Check your email for hosting login credentials
2. Look for "cPanel login" or "hosting dashboard" link
3. Login with your credentials


STEP 3: UPLOAD FILES USING FILE MANAGER
----------------------------------------
Once in cPanel or File Manager:

1. Navigate to "public_html" or "www" folder
   (This is where your website files go)

2. DELETE any existing files in there:
   - index.html (old one)
   - Any placeholder pages

3. UPLOAD YOUR FILES:
   - Click "Upload" button
   - Select all files from C:\Users\Admin\HelmickUnderground
   - Wait for upload to complete (should take 1-2 minutes)

4. VERIFY the files are there:
   - You should see: index.html, about.html, services.html, contact.html, styles.css, script.js


STEP 4: UPLOAD USING FTP (ALTERNATIVE METHOD)
----------------------------------------------
If you prefer using FTP software (more reliable for multiple files):

SOFTWARE OPTIONS (pick one):
- FileZilla (free): https://filezilla-project.org/
- WinSCP (free): https://winscp.net/
- Cyberduck (free): https://cyberduck.io/

FTP CREDENTIALS (get from your hosting provider):
- Host/Server: Usually ftp.helmickunderground.com or helmickunderground.com
- Username: Your hosting username (check hosting email)
- Password: Your hosting password
- Port: 21 (standard FTP) or 22 (SFTP/secure)

STEPS:
1. Install FTP software (FileZilla recommended)
2. Open FileZilla
3. Click File > Site Manager > New Site
4. Enter your FTP details:
   - Host: ftp.helmickunderground.com
   - Port: 21
   - Protocol: FTP or SFTP
   - Username: (from hosting)
   - Password: (from hosting)
5. Click "Connect"
6. Navigate to "public_html" folder on the right side
7. Drag and drop all your files from left side (your computer) to right side (server)

STEP 6: ENABLE HTTPS/SSL (IMPORTANT FOR SECURITY)
--------------------------------------------------
Your website should use HTTPS (secure).

In cPanel:
1. Find "SSL/TLS Status" or "Let's Encrypt SSL"
2. Click "Run AutoSSL" or "Install SSL Certificate"
3. Select your domain: helmickunderground.com
4. Click "Install" or "Enable"
5. Wait 2-5 minutes for installation

Most hosting providers offer FREE SSL certificates (Let's Encrypt).


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
PROBLEM: "I can't find my hosting login"
SOLUTION: 
- Check email inbox for "hosting account" or "welcome" from your domain registrar
- If using GoDaddy, reset password at godaddy.com
- Contact your hosting provider's support

PROBLEM: "Files uploaded but website doesn't show"
SOLUTION:
- Make sure files are in "public_html" not a subfolder
- Rename index.html exactly (case-sensitive)
- Clear browser cache
- Wait 10-15 minutes for DNS propagation

PROBLEM: "Contact form not working on live site"
SOLUTION:
- This should work automatically since we use Web3Forms API
- Test by submitting a form
- Check HelmickUnderground@gmail.com for test email

PROBLEM: "Images not showing"
SOLUTION:
- Make sure "images" folder is uploaded
- Check file names match exactly (case-sensitive)
- Upload equipment photos to images/ folder
