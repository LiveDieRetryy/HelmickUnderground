# Equipment Images

Please add your equipment photos to this folder with the following filenames:

1. **directional-drilling.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of directional drilling equipment
2. **excavating.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of excavator
3. **utility-locating.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of utility locating equipment/tools
4. **trenching.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of trenching equipment
5. **plowing.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of plowing equipment
6. **concrete.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of concrete work/equipment
7. **fiber-blowing.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of fiber blowing equipment
8. **line-pulling.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of line pulling equipment
9. **skid-loader.png** (or .jpg) - Photo of skid loader

## Image Specifications:
- Format: PNG or JPG (PNG preferred, automatically falls back to JPG)
- Recommended size: 800x600px minimum
- Aspect ratio: 4:3 or 16:9 works best
- File size: Keep under 500KB each for fast loading

## Tips:
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Show equipment in action when possible
- Ensure equipment is clean and clearly visible
- Horizontal (landscape) orientation preferred

Once you add your images, the website will automatically display them on the Services page!
