Expert Excavation Services

With over 38 years of experience, Helmick Underground provides comprehensive excavating services for residential, commercial, and municipal projects throughout Iowa. Our modern fleet of excavation equipment and skilled operators ensure efficient, safe, and precise excavation for any underground utility project.

Our Excavating Capabilities

  • Utility Line Installation: Excavation for water, sewer, gas, and electric lines
  • Site Preparation: Grading and earth moving for new construction
  • Foundation Work: Basement and foundation excavation
  • Drainage Solutions: French drains, catch basins, and stormwater management
  • Demolition Support: Removal of old utilities and structures
  • Trenching: Precise trenching for utility installation

Why Choose Our Excavating Services?

  • Modern, well-maintained excavation equipment
  • Experienced, certified operators
  • Safe excavation practices and OSHA compliance
  • Efficient project completion
  • Professional site cleanup and restoration
  • Proper utility locating before any excavation

Complete Project Management

From initial site assessment to final grading and restoration, we handle every aspect of your excavation project. We coordinate with utility companies, obtain necessary permits, and ensure all work meets local codes and regulations. Our commitment to quality means we leave your site in better condition than we found it.
