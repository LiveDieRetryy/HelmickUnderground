Concrete Services

Quality Concrete Work for Utility Infrastructure

Professional Concrete Services

Helmick Underground provides quality concrete services essential to underground utility projects. From utility vaults and equipment pads to sidewalk and driveway restoration, our concrete work meets industry standards and local building codes. With decades of experience, we understand the importance of proper concrete installation for the longevity and reliability of underground utility systems.

Our Concrete Services Include:

  • Utility Vaults: Telecommunications and electrical access vaults
  • Equipment Pads: Transformer, generator, and HVAC pads
  • Manhole Installation: Sewer and utility access manholes
  • Sidewalk Restoration: Removal and replacement after utility work
  • Driveway Restoration: Concrete driveway repair and replacement
  • Curb and Gutter: Street and parking lot concrete work
  • Foundation Work: Small foundations for utility structures

Quality Materials and Workmanship

  • Proper concrete mix for Iowa weather conditions
  • Appropriate reinforcement and rebar placement
  • Proper curing techniques for maximum strength
  • Attention to grades and drainage
  • Smooth, professional finishes
  • ADA-compliant work when required

Complete Project Restoration

Underground utility projects often require concrete removal and replacement. We handle the entire process, from careful removal of existing concrete to professional installation of new concrete that matches surrounding surfaces. Our goal is to leave your property looking better than before we started, with quality concrete work that will last for decades.

Experience You Can Trust

As a full-service underground utility contractor, we understand how concrete work integrates with utility installation projects. This expertise allows us to coordinate concrete work efficiently with other project phases, saving you time and money. Whether it's a small equipment pad or extensive sidewalk restoration, we deliver quality concrete work on schedule.
