Plowing

Advanced Plowing for Underground Cable Installation

Efficient Underground Cable Installation

Cable plowing is an efficient method of installing underground cables and utility lines with minimal surface disruption. Helmick Underground utilizes specialized plowing equipment that simultaneously opens the ground, installs the cable or conduit, and closes the ground in one continuous operation. This method is faster and less invasive than traditional trenching for many applications.

Plowing Applications

  • Fiber Optic Cables: High-speed internet and telecommunications infrastructure
  • Electrical Service: Underground power distribution and service lines
  • Irrigation Systems: Agricultural and landscape watering systems
  • Cable TV: Coaxial cable distribution networks
  • Telephone Lines: Communication cable installation
  • Control Cables: Security, lighting, and automation systems

Benefits of Cable Plowing

  • Minimal surface disruption and landscaping damage
  • Faster installation than traditional trenching
  • Cost-effective for long cable runs
  • Ideal for existing landscaped areas
  • Precise depth control for proper cable placement
  • Reduced restoration and cleanup time

Professional Installation Standards

Our experienced operators understand proper cable burial depths and installation techniques to ensure long-lasting, reliable service. We follow industry standards and local codes for all installations. Before plowing, we conduct thorough utility locating to prevent damage to existing infrastructure. The result is a professional installation that protects your investment while preserving your property.

Versatile Equipment Fleet

With various sizes of plowing equipment, we can handle projects from residential cable installations to large-scale commercial developments. Our team has the experience to work in different soil conditions and terrain types throughout Iowa, ensuring successful cable installation regardless of site challenges.
