Utility Locating

Safely Identify Underground Infrastructure Before You Dig

Professional Utility Locating Services

Before any excavation begins, it's critical to know what's underground. Helmick Underground provides professional utility locating services to accurately identify and mark existing underground infrastructure. Our advanced locating equipment and experienced technicians help prevent costly damage, project delays, and safety hazards.

What We Locate

  • Electric Lines: Power distribution and service lines
  • Gas Lines: Natural gas and propane distribution
  • Water Lines: Municipal water and private well lines
  • Sewer Lines: Sanitary and storm sewer systems
  • Telecommunications: Fiber optic and copper communication lines
  • Cable TV: Coaxial cable and related infrastructure
  • Unknown Utilities: Abandoned or undocumented lines

Our Locating Process

  • Site assessment and utility records review
  • Advanced electromagnetic locating equipment
  • Color-coded marking per industry standards
  • Detailed documentation and mapping
  • Coordination with utility companies

Why Utility Locating Is Essential

Striking underground utilities can result in service disruptions, expensive repairs, project delays, injuries, or even fatalities. Professional utility locating protects your workers, your budget, and your timeline. Iowa law requires calling 811 before digging, and we work in compliance with all state and federal regulations to ensure safe excavation.

Comprehensive Safety Approach

We don't just mark utilities - we help you understand what's beneath your project site. Our detailed reports and on-site consultations give you the information needed to excavate safely and efficiently. With decades of experience in Iowa's underground infrastructure, we know what to look for and how to protect it.
