Cable and Line Pulling

Expert Cable Installation for Telecommunications and Utility Projects

Professional Cable and Line Pulling Services

Helmick Underground provides expert cable and line pulling services for telecommunications and utility projects throughout Iowa. Using specialized pulling equipment and proven techniques, we safely install cables through conduit systems while maintaining proper tension limits and protecting cable integrity. Our experienced crew has the knowledge and equipment to handle any cable pulling project.

Types of Cables We Pull

  • Fiber Optic Cables: Single mode and multimode fiber for data networks
  • Copper Communication: Telephone and data cables
  • Coaxial Cable: Cable TV and broadband distribution
  • Power Cables: Electrical distribution and service lines
  • Control Cables: Security, automation, and monitoring systems
  • Multiple Cables: Simultaneous multi-cable installations

Our Pulling Capabilities

  • Powered cable pulling equipment for heavy loads
  • Tension monitoring to prevent cable damage
  • Proper cable lubrication techniques
  • Complex conduit system navigation
  • Long-distance cable installations
  • Vertical and horizontal cable runs

Safety and Cable Protection

Proper cable installation requires careful attention to manufacturer specifications and industry standards. We monitor pulling tensions to ensure cables remain within their rated limits, preventing damage that could compromise performance or lifespan. Our team uses appropriate pulling grips, swivels, and lubrication to protect cables during installation. Every cable pull is planned and executed to deliver a reliable, long-lasting installation.

Experience Across All Project Sizes

From single residential fiber connections to large commercial telecommunications installations, we have the experience and equipment to complete your cable pulling project professionally. Our knowledge of Iowa's utility infrastructure and local requirements ensures smooth project execution. We coordinate with other trades and utility companies to keep projects on schedule.

Complete Installation Services

As a full-service underground contractor, we handle every phase of cable installation - from conduit installation to cable pulling to final termination and testing. This comprehensive approach eliminates coordination issues and ensures a quality installation from start to finish.
