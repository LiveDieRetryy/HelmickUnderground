Directional Drilling

Precision Underground Installation with Minimal Surface Disruption

What is Directional Drilling?

Directional drilling, also known as horizontal directional drilling (HDD), is a trenchless method of installing underground utilities with minimal surface disruption. Using advanced drilling equipment, we create precise pathways beneath roads, landscapes, and structures without the need for extensive excavation.

Benefits of Directional Drilling

  • Minimal Surface Disruption: Preserves landscaping, driveways, and existing infrastructure
  • Cost-Effective: Reduces restoration costs and project timelines
  • Environmentally Friendly: Less soil displacement and environmental impact
  • Versatile Applications: Ideal for crossing under obstacles like roads, rivers, and buildings
  • Precise Placement: Accurate utility installation using advanced tracking technology

Our Directional Drilling Services Include:

  • Fiber optic cable installation
  • Water and sewer line installation
  • Gas and electric utility lines
  • Telecommunications infrastructure
  • Conduit installation for future utilities
  • River and road crossings
