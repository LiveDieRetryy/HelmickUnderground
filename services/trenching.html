Trenching

Efficient Trenching Solutions for Utility Line Installation

Professional Trenching for Utility Installation

Helmick Underground provides efficient trenching services for installing underground utilities and infrastructure. Our modern trenching equipment and experienced operators deliver precise, cost-effective trenching for projects of any size. From residential utility connections to large commercial developments, we have the expertise to complete your trenching project safely and on schedule.

Trenching Applications

  • Utility Line Installation: Water, sewer, gas, and electric lines
  • Telecommunications: Fiber optic and communication cable trenching
  • Drainage Systems: French drains and stormwater management
  • Irrigation Systems: Agricultural and landscape irrigation
  • Foundation Drainage: Perimeter drain tile installation
  • Cable and Conduit: Trenching for protected cable runs

Our Trenching Capabilities

  • Variable depth trenching from shallow to deep excavation
  • Narrow trenches for minimal surface disturbance
  • Wide trenches for multiple utility installations
  • Rocky soil and challenging terrain experience
  • Precision depth control for proper utility placement
  • Proper bedding and backfill procedures

Safety and Quality Standards

Every trenching project begins with proper utility locating to prevent damage to existing infrastructure. We follow OSHA safety guidelines for trench safety, including proper shoring and sloping when required. Our team ensures proper backfilling and compaction to prevent future settling, and we restore your property to its original condition or better.

Efficient Project Completion

With decades of trenching experience throughout Iowa, we understand local soil conditions and utility requirements. Our equipment fleet allows us to match the right trencher to your specific project needs, ensuring efficient completion while minimizing costs. We coordinate with utility companies and follow all local codes and regulations.
